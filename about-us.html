About Us

TULYE RESTAURANT ONLINE BOOKING AND RESERVATION

Our story began in 2008 when four brothers opened the first Tulye Restaurant in lusaka, cairo rd, Zambia.

Since then, we've become known for our excellent food that caters to zambians and all people from around the world.

Our customer service is friendly and our restaurants provide a warm environment where our customers can enjoy a perfect meal made with love

The very heart of Tulye restaurants are the memories you make with Us. They all add up to one great story: the stuff that legends are made of.
